    Moldova

    Introduction

    Republic of Moldova is a small landlocked country located in Eastern Europe between Romania and Ukraine.

    The current population is around 2 million and the official language is Romanian. A big majority of the population can speak both Romanian and Russian. Moldovans are mostly religious, we follow Christianity, specifically Eastern Orthodox.

    Interesting Facts

    • The hit song Dragostea Din Tei, internationally more known as Numa Numa, is performed by a Moldovian band - O-Zone.
    • The Epic Sax Guy internet meme is from Moldova’s performance at Eurovision in 2010, the band SunStroke Project with their song Run Away.
    • Moldova is the poorest country in Europe with the highest declining population rate. Thank you, politicians.
    • We have the biggest underground wine cellar in the world. The Mileștii Mici cellars extends for 200 kilometres, of which only 55 kilometres are currently in use.
