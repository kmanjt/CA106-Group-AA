    Introduction - Food

    Nigeria has a vast number of traditional dishes. With so many different ethnic communities, Nigeria has a rich culinary tradition. Nigeria has one of the best cuisines in the world. Using a mixture of spices, herbes and palm and groundnut oil, Nigerians have created delicious dishes with splendid aromas.

    (Oyibo, 2020)

    Some popular dishes include:

    • Rice dishes such as: Jollof Rice, Fried rice, Coconut rice, Rice and stew.
    • There are also dishes called “swallow”. Swallow is the general term for food balls that are swallowed without chewing. Eba, Amala, Pounded Yam and Semolina are examples of swallow. Swallows are eaten with soup. There are hundreds of different types of soups, for example ogbono, bitter leaf soup, groundnut soup, cocoyam soup, egusi and okra soup.
