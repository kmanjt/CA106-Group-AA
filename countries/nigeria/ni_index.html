    Introduction To Nigeria

    The west African country, Nigeria, is a multicultural country with a huge population of around 200 million and over 350 unique tribes. It is a West African country. It is located on the western coast of Africa, with neighbouring countries such as Benin, Niger and Cameroon. The diversity in Nigeria is unimaginable. Nigeria is one of the most linguistically diverse countries in the world! There are over 500 different languages spoken, however the official language is English. There are also many different religions practiced in Nigeria, the most popular being Islam and Christianity.
