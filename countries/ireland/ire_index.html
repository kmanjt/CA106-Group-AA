    Introduction

    Ireland is located on the west side of Europe right beside the UK on its west side. Its population is just shy of 5 million people with numbers still rising ever since the end of the famine. The native language in Ireland is Irish although it is on the decline with English being the main language spoken for some time. Ireland is mainly a christian country but there are many other religions that are practised in Ireland.


    Facts about Ireland

    • Guinness originated in Ireland and when starting was granted a 9000 year lease
    • The official language in Ireland is Irish
    • The population is 4.9 million
    • Ireland has won the Eurovision song contest 7 times. The most of any country.
